### 3mm White LED, lf=20mA

* Forward Voltage: 3.2v (min), 3.4v (typ)
* Dominant Wavelength: 5000K (min), 6500K (typ)
* MCD: 16000 (min), 20000 (typ)
* Reverse current, Vr=5V: 10uA (max)
* Power Angle: 20-25deg
